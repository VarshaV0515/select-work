The Strategic Context

Even before the pandemic, Walmart's pharmacy business was exploring new ways to modernize the customer experience. Initial research and planning for a curbside pickup channel began earlier, aimed at creating a more convenient and accessible service for all pharmacy customers in an increasingly digital world.

When the COVID-19 pandemic transformed this strategic project into an urgent public health imperative, the team was able to accelerate its work. The core user problem was no longer just about convenience; it was about ensuring safe, reliable access to essential medication.

As a Senior Product Designer, my role was to derive and translate research insights into a UX flow for the curbside pickup channel of Online Walmart Pharmacy services. I collaborated closely with cross-functional horizontal design, research, and engineering teams across the U.S. and India to ensure the user-centric flows aligned with technical integrations on backend & design consistent UI with Walmart’s omni-channel design system. A key part of my work involved defining the workflows for new, contactless Rx fulfilment preference and HIPAA-compliant payment systems. This rapid execution ultimately delivered critical safety for users and drove measurable growth for Walmart’s pharmacy business.
